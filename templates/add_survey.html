Add Google Form Surveys

Instructions

1. Click the "Add Survey" button to add a new survey.


2. Enter the survey name (no spaces) in the first field.


3. In Google Forms, Be sure to create "Name" and "ID" fields at the top of the survey.


4. Prefill each of these with dummy values (e.g. "Name", and "ID number")


5. In "Edit" mode, select "more" in the upper right corner (three pip menu)


6. Select "Get prefilled link" and copy the generated link

7. Paste the link in the URL field of this form.


8. Click "Submit" to add the survey to the list of surveys.
