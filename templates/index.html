Instructions for adding surveys

  • Enter the survey name (no spaces) in the first field.
  • In Google Forms, be sure to create "Name" and "ID" fields at the top of the survey.
  • Prefill each of these with the dummy values: "Sample Name", and "Sample ID".
  • In "Edit" mode, select "more" in the upper right corner (three pip menu).
  • Select "Get prefilled link" and copy the generated link.
  • Paste the link in the URL field of this form.
  • Click "Submit Survey" to add the survey to the list of surveys.

Instructions for EmotiBit

Important: Subject must complete the "Subject Information" survey task before the EmotiBit can be started.

  • Fit subject with the Emotibit (See supplementary material for fitting device onto subject).

  • Press "Start Collecting Baseline" to start the EmotiBit streamer and begin collecting baseline data.
  • NOTE: EmotiBit will run continuously until "Stop Emotibit" is clicked ("Post-Test" at bottom of page).
